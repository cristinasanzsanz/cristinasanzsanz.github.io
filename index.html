I am a Computational/Theoretical Chemist at Autonoma University Madrid, where I teach and do research in Quantum Chemistry and Quantum/Classical Dynamics. My research interests lie in the study of Chemical Reactions, Spectroscopy and LASER driven control of chemical reactivity mainly of Astrophysical interest.

Updates:

  • April 2024: I joined University College London (3 months) as a Research Visitor thanks to a Salvador de Madariaga project of the Ministry of Science, Innovation and Universities

  • September 2023: I was promoted to Associate Professor (tenured) at Autonoma University Madrid.
